Step 1: Make a chain for a foundation.

Step 2: Wrap the yarn over your hook once.

Step 3: Insert hook into the fourth chain from the hook.

Step 4: Wrap the yarn over and pull through the chain. You should have three loops on your hook.

Step 5: Yarn over and pull through the first 2 loops on the hook.

Step 6: Yarn over again and pull through the remaining 2 loops. This creates one double crochet.

Step 7: Put one double crochet in each chain until the end.

Step 8: At the end of every row, chain 3 stitches as a turning chain.

Step 9: Cut the yarn and pull the tail through the remaining loop on the hook.
